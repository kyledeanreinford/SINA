South Inglewood Neighborhood Association

We live here and we love it.

Welcome to South Inglewood! We love our little community tucked away in East Nashville, and we invite you to experience it with us. We have great restaurants and bars, a great community center, tons of churches and we are changing very, very fast.

Consider this your resource site for all we have in South Inglewood and consider joining our fight to become a stronger, more resilient community by attending our business meetings and social functions. If you'd like to become a member, scroll down for details. And if you have any questions or input, please feel free to reach out to us!

Our current neighborhood map is below, but we're not exclusive - if you want in and you're close by, let us know!

Membership


An annual membership helps support the South Inglewood Neighborhood Association by providing resources for maintenance of the website, communications, volunteer and social events. Membership is open to residents and/or owners of commercial or residential property within South Inglewood.

Recommended yearly membership is $20, but if that's a barrier in any way, please let us know. You can join below.
